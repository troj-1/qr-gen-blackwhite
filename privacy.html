Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account on QR Star, we collect:

  • Name — your display name
  • Email address — used for account login and (optionally) product updates
  • Password — stored as a one-way hash, never in plain text
  • Referral source — how you found us (optional)
  • Newsletter preference — whether you opted in to receive updates

When you use QR Star, we automatically collect:

  • Usage events — QR code types generated, design styles used, download actions (stored locally in your browser)
  • Analytics — anonymous usage patterns to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the QR Star service
  • To create and manage your account
  • To track your QR code generation history (when logged in)
  • To send product updates and tips (only if you opted in)
  • To improve our service through aggregated, anonymized usage data

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Your account data, generation history, and usage analytics remain on your device. We do not transmit your data to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

QR Star uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Cookies

QR Star itself does not use cookies. However, Google AdSense may set cookies to serve personalized advertisements. These cookies are governed by Google's privacy policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you individually.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your data (available in your account settings)
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

8. Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, log in to QR Star and use the "Delete Account" option in your account settings, or email us at privacy@qrstar.io.

9. Children's Privacy

QR Star is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@qrstar.io or visit our contact page.
