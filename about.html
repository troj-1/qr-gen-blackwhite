About QR Star

A free QR code generator built for everyone.

QR Star is a free, browser-based QR code generator designed to make creating QR codes simple, fast, and customizable. No sign-up required to start — just type your content and generate.

Why QR Star?

Most QR code generators offer basic functionality with limited customization. QR Star was built to give you:

  • 14 content types — from simple URLs to vCards, WiFi credentials, payment links, and event invitations
  • 5 module styles — square, rounded, dots, extra-rounded, and diamond patterns
  • 3 corner styles — customize how the finder patterns look
  • Custom borders — with sharp, soft, or rounded edges and adjustable thickness
  • Any color — foreground, background, and border colors are fully customizable
  • Instant download — save as PNG with one click

How It Works

Everything runs in your browser. Your data never leaves your device. When you create a QR code:

  1. Select a content type and enter your data
  2. Customize colors, styles, and border
  3. Click "Generate" to create your QR code
  4. Download the PNG or copy it to your clipboard

Built with Care

QR Star is built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies, no bloat. This means:

  • Lightning fast load times
  • Works on any device with a browser
  • No tracking scripts beyond what's needed for ads
  • Your data stays local — nothing is sent to a server

Free Forever

QR Star is and will always be free. Anonymous users can generate up to 3 QR codes per hour. Create a free account for unlimited generation and to save your QR code history.
