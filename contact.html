Frequently Asked Questions

Is QR Star really free?

Yes. QR Star is completely free to use. Anonymous users can generate up to 3 QR codes per hour. Sign up for free to get unlimited generation and save your history.

Do I need an account?

No. You can generate QR codes without an account. Creating a free account gives you unlimited generation, QR code history, and the ability to save your designs.

What types of QR codes can I create?

QR Star supports 14 types: URL, WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Text, Email, SMS, vCard, Location, WiFi, PayPal, Bitcoin, and Events. Each type has custom foreground/background colors, 5 module styles, and 3 corner styles.

Can I use QR codes commercially?

Absolutely. QR codes you generate are yours to use for any personal or commercial purpose.

How do I delete my account?

Log in, go to your account settings, and click "Delete Account." This permanently removes all your data. You can also email privacy@qrstar.io.

My QR code isn't scanning. What's wrong?

Try these steps: ensure sufficient contrast between foreground and background colors, use a large enough size (300px+), and avoid overly complex content. Test on multiple devices before distributing.
